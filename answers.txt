Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git makes it really easy to collaborate with others on a project. There is also version control which allows people to go back to previous iterations of their code.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be used in Marketing. In marketing you would want to track customer behavior and be able to analyze what works and what doesn't. You can use data science to target specific audiences and tailor your product to them.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging is when you take two branches and combine them into one whereas rebasing takes a branch and adds the new portions one by one on the lastest commit of the base branch.
